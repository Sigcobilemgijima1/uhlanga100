WHO IS UHLANGA BOOKS


UHLANGA BOOKS IS A PUBLISHING COMPANY BASED IN KHAYELITSHA.IT WAS FORMED BY A VISIONARY AND SERIAL ENTREPRENEUR NAMED ZIMKITHA ZILO.


Uhlanga Books was established in 2019. They specialise in self-publishing, AND have published 9books so far,

all of which are amazing in terms of quality and content.

They publish all genres such as:

* poetry

* fiction

* non-fiction

* short story

* adult erotic fiction

* romance

* study guides



other than hiding in books I feel like reading made me a more aware person

it boosted my confidence level and helped me become an encyclopedia of a rondom facts

When I moved to Cpt from the Free State ,I stayed in KHayelitsha

I experienced children loiter in the streets with nothing to do

the streets flooded with alcohol bottles and cigarette stompies,that was culture shock of my life!

I would just close my eyes and envison the whole streets flooded with books and children reading instead of finishing off alcohol bottles and pretending to smoke

So I am trying to bring my imaginary book flooding to life by having children`s book club around Khayelitsha.

I get my books from the center for the books ,I have no shortage of books,which is amazing.I also read in the pediatric ward of Khayelitsha District Hospital

I am hoping to have these booknics in every township,however funds or rather resources are limiting me to just Makhaya and Kuyasa

Khayelitsha Book Festival

As mentioned before I want to flud the streets of Khayelitsha with books , and what better way than to use September book week to close off a street and have books everywhere, I want to introduce reading for pleasure in the township and I want to encourage writers to publish their work and have published prominent authors share their journeys of being published authors , I want being a writer to be a career option. I just want the culture of reading to be cool and be a thing. I know that there are book festivals happening eg. the Open book festival, I got tickets for some of teens to come to experience the festival and it turned out to be an academic debate space , wich was lovely , however , I did not feel like that sold the idea of reading as cool , wich is why I want to host a festival that will speak to what I want books and reading to be.
